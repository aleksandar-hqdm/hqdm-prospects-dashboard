Method and sources

All figures from live DataForSEO pulls on 2026-06-03: ranked keywords, Google Ads search volume, live Maps results across a nine-point metro grid, live search results with AI Overview detection, ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity and Claude responses, an on-page crawl, the Google Business Profile, and the backlink profile. No Google Analytics or Search Console access was available for this prospect, so traffic and lead figures are modeled from search volume and published benchmark rates, and are labeled as such. Lead estimates are directional, meant to size the opportunity, and should be validated with tracking once it is in place.
